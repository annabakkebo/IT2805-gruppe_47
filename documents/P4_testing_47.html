Our client: La Escuela

Our client, Arne Brillestad, is the headmaster at La Escuela. La Escuela is a private elementary school in Norway. The school has creativity and sustainability as their main focuses.

Testing Document

The testing document is a Google Form, and focuses on how easy it is to navigate the website, how accesible the information is, what worked well and what could be improved. The user is also asked to give up their age and if they would send their children to the school, after seeing the website. Lastly we ask about what kind of unit they used to access the website.
