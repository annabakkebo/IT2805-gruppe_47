Our client: La Escuela

Our client, Arne Brillestad, is the headmaster at La Escuela. La Escuela is a private elementary school in Norway. The school has creativity and sustainability as their main focuses.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of the website is to have a platform of information to mainly parents. Through this platform the teachers, the principal and admin at the school can publish necessary information for the parents and pupils to read. The most important information that is relevant to all parents and pupils will be posted on the main page. There will be a search bar where users can search for relevant information.

The website will also contain contact information for all the employees at the school.

Audience

The website is aimed mainly towards parents of current or future pupils. Pupils can also use the site, but it will mostly be directed towards the parents. It will function as the main information hub from the school to its users. The parents will here be able to retrieve important information about the school as well as be able to apply for their children to attend the school.

Content of the website and organization

  1. Front page
    • Here we will have the major news from the school. This will be news that are applicable to all parents independent of what classes their children are in.
  2. About
    • This will be the general information about the school. This page will not be updated as frequently as the information on the front page.
    • Goals for the school
    • The different services the school offers. For instance library, rides, school nurse etc.
  3. Application tab
    • Here parents can apply for their child to attend this school.
    • There will also be a option here to look at when the parents and their child can come visit the school.
  4. Contact
    • Contact information of the different employees at the school.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

Functional requirements

  • The user will be able to change language from english to norwegian and vice versa. The main focus will though be in Norwegian, and the english will be a bit less exhaustive.
  • The website should be able to accept applications.
  • The menu tab must work.
  • The user should be able to retrieve the contact information to the different employees.

Non-functional requirements

  • When the user clicks the norwegian or british flag the language changes to norwegian or english.
  • The user should be able to write in a form to apply.
  • When clicking on the contact tab the user should get the contact of all the employees. The user should also be able to search for the different employees.
  • The logo of the school should be on every page.
