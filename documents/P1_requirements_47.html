Our client: La Escuela

Our client, Arne Brillestad, is the headmaster at La Escuela. La Escuela is an elementary school in Norway.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of the website is to have a platform of information to both pupils and parents. Through this platform the teachers, the principal and admin at the school can publish necessary information for the parents and pupils to read. The most important information that is relevant to all parents and pupils will be posted on the main page. In addition there will also be pages for each class where the teacher will post information that is relevant for the pupils and parents of that class. It will also be possible to subscribe to the different information channels.

The website will also contain contact information for all the employees at the school.

Audience

The web site has mainly two users; pupils and parents of pupils. It shall function as the main information hub from the school to its users. Here they can go to check for information on what sort of courses the school offers and possible events.
