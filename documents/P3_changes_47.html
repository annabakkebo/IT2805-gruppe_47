Our client: La Escuela

Our client, Arne Brillestad, is the headmaster at La Escuela. La Escuela is a private elementary school in Norway. The school has creativity and sustainability as their main focuses.

The changes we have made

There have been no changes made concerning the amount of webpages, but there were many design and structural changes. These changes are summarised below.

The application.js was removed entirely, as the admission/index.html handled all of the data for the application scheme. The languageChange.js was also removed entirely, because we decided to use latin fill text and we did not find a satisfactory way of implementing this translation function. Changed the name of the js file slideshow.js to slide.js. Added calendar.css and contact.css. Added contactInfo.js and data.js.
