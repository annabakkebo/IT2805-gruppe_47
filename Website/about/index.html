Information about the school

Here we will have a lot of information about the school. Exactly what information that is needed here is up to the school to decide. Some of the information that we would recommend on this side is information about who can apply for the school. We thought that the child has to be at least 3 years old, and maximum 20 years old.

General information about the school will also be here. For instance the goal of the school and other stuff. We've decided to just add filltext here so the user can get an idea of what it will look like
